Rich Dad

Rich Dad Poor Dad is mainly about achieving financial freedom through financial literacy, contrasting the mindset of a financially savvy "rich dad" with Kiyosaki's educated but financially struggling "poor dad," emphasizing that the rich make money work for them by acquiring assets (like real estate, businesses) rather than just working for a salary, and shifting from an employee mentality to an entrepreneurial one.
